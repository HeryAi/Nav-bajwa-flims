ABOUT NAV BAJWA FILMS

From Punjab to the World.
Stories Without Borders.

Established in 2016, Nav Bajwa Films is a film production and entertainment company dedicated to creating compelling stories for audiences across Pollywood, Bollywood, and international markets.

About Us

Nav Bajwa Films was founded with a vision to elevate Punjabi and Indian cinema beyond traditional boundaries. Since 2016, the company has been actively developing and producing content that combines strong storytelling, cultural authenticity, and global appeal.

Over the years, Nav Bajwa Films has built a reputation for delivering meaningful entertainment while continuously exploring opportunities in film production, international collaborations, digital content, and global distribution.

With roots firmly grounded in Punjab and a forward-looking international perspective, the company seeks to connect regional cinema with worldwide audiences.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Cinema Beyond Entertainment

At Nav Bajwa Films, we believe cinema is more than entertainment. It is a powerful medium for cultural exchange, tourism promotion, economic growth, and creative innovation.

Through feature films, digital projects, and strategic partnerships with filmmakers, studios, distributors, film commissions, and OTT platforms, we create stories that resonate across cultures and generations.

OUR VISION

To become a globally recognized entertainment company that bridges Pollywood, Bollywood, and international cinema, creating stories that inspire, entertain, and connect audiences worldwide.

OUR MISSION

To develop and produce exceptional film and digital content, foster international partnerships, promote cultural exchange, and contribute to the growth of the global entertainment industry through innovative storytelling.
