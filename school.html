ACTING & TECHNICAL SCHOOL

Building The Future Of Cinema.

A new platform for aspiring actors, filmmakers, writers, directors, cinematographers, editors, and creative professionals is on its way.

ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Learn From Industry Experience

The Nav Bajwa Films Acting & Technical School is being developed to nurture the next generation of talent in Punjabi, Hindi, and international cinema.

Through practical training, mentorship, on-set learning, and real-world production experience, students will gain industry-ready skills for modern filmmaking.

PRACTICAL LEARNING

Learn Beyond The Classroom

Students will gain exposure to real film productions, industry workflows, professional equipment, and mentorship from experienced filmmakers and creative professionals.

FOUNDER'S VISION

Inspired By Nav Bajwa's Journey

Founded on the creative experience of filmmaker, actor, director, and producer Nav Bajwa, the school aims to bridge the gap between education and the entertainment industry.

Students will learn contemporary filmmaking practices that serve both regional and global audiences.
