COMPANY OVERVIEW

M/S Nav Bajwa Films Private Limited is a dynamic Punjabi film production company based in Punjab, India, since 2016 dedicated to creating high-quality cinema with global appeal. Founded by actor, director, writer, producer, and singer Navjot Singh Bajwa, popularly known as Nav Bajwa, the company focuses on blending strong storytelling with modern filmmaking techniques while celebrating Punjabi culture, music, and entertainment. The key stakeholders of the company are Navjot Singh Bajwa and Inderjeet Kaur.

LEADERSHIP

Leadership Team

Navjot Singh Bajwa (Nav Bajwa)

Actor, Writer, Director & Producer

Under the creative leadership of Nav Bajwa, the company has established a strong identity in the Punjabi film industry through innovative and genre-diverse projects. Nav Bajwa began his journey in the entertainment industry after winning the dance reality show Aaja Nach Le in 2007 and later made his acting debut with Pure Punjabi in 2012. Over the years, he has earned recognition for his versatility and forward-thinking approach as an actor, director, writer, and producer.

Nav Bajwa Films has built a reputation for introducing fresh concepts and technically ambitious productions to Punjabi cinema. The company’s notable projects include Ishqaa, Kitty Party, Madhaniya, Advocate Chauhan, Raduaa, and Raduaa Returns. The film Raduaa became one of the pioneering Punjabi science fiction films based on time travel, while its sequel Raduaa Returns further expanded the scale of Punjabi sci-fi cinema through futuristic storytelling and advanced visual effects. Through these projects, the company continues to promote meaningful entertainment while exploring contemporary themes and cinematic styles within regional cinema.


Inderjeet Kaur (Naman Hanjra)

Actor, Director & Producer

Inderjeet Kaur, professionally known as Naman Hanjra, is a key stakeholder of Nav Bajwa Films and an influential contributor to the company’s creative and strategic vision. With extensive experience in the Punjabi entertainment industry, she brings a strong understanding of audience preferences, content development, and cinematic storytelling. Her involvement extends beyond performance, encompassing project evaluation, creative planning, script assessment, and content development.

As a creative strategist, she plays an important role in identifying commercially viable and culturally relevant stories that align with the company’s long-term vision. Her ability to balance artistic excellence with market potential contributes significantly to the selection and development of projects that resonate with both domestic and international audiences. Through her dedication to quality-driven content and innovative storytelling, she continues to support the company’s mission of expanding the global footprint of Punjabi cinema.


Amitoj Garg

CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER ( C.E.O. )

Amitoj Garg serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Nav Bajwa Films and is responsible for driving the company’s business strategy, operational excellence, and long-term growth initiatives. He oversees corporate planning, business development, financial coordination, production management, and strategic partnerships, ensuring the successful execution of projects across all stages of development and production.

With a strong focus on industry networking and international expansion, he actively works toward establishing collaborations with film commissions, production houses, distributors, OTT platforms, investors, and government agencies. His leadership plays a vital role in exploring global co-production opportunities, film tourism initiatives, international distribution networks, and cross-border creative partnerships.

Amitoj Garg is also instrumental in strengthening the company’s corporate structure, enhancing operational efficiency, and positioning Nav Bajwa Films as a globally competitive content creation and production enterprise. His vision is centered on building sustainable growth, fostering international relationships, and creating new avenues for Punjabi and Indian cinema within the global entertainment ecosystem.


Shamsher Singh Bajwa

Executive Producer

Shamsher Singh Bajwa manages the day-to-day production operations of films. He coordinates schedules, locations, crew, equipment, permits, logistics, and on-set activities, ensuring the smooth execution of production plans. His role focuses on implementing production requirements efficiently, maintaining timelines, managing resources, and supporting seamless operations throughout the filmmaking process.
