Live Events & Entertainment

Beyond film production, Nav Bajwa Films is expanding into live entertainment, cultural experiences, audience engagement platforms, and brand-driven events that connect cinema directly with communities and global Punjabi audiences.

EVENT EXPERIENCES

Entertainment Across Every Platform

Singing Concerts

Live music events featuring Punjabi singers, film soundtrack showcases, promotional music tours, unplugged performances, and artist collaborations. These events create direct fan engagement while promoting film music and emerging talent.

Comedy Shows

Stand-up comedy events, Punjabi comedy tours, celebrity-hosted entertainment evenings, and family-focused live performances designed to celebrate Punjabi culture through humor and storytelling.

Film Festivals

Film screenings, red-carpet premieres, international festival participation, independent cinema showcases, and Punjabi film celebrations that promote regional storytelling on national and global platforms. Nav Bajwa has been associated with multiple Punjabi films across drama, romance, comedy, thriller, and science-fiction genres.
