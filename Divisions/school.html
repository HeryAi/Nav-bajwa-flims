ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Learn from Industry Professionals

A new platform for aspiring actors, filmmakers, writers, directors, cinematographers, editors, and creative professionals is on its way.

The Nav Bajwa Films Acting & Technical School is being developed with a vision to nurture the next generation of talent in Punjabi, Hindi, and international cinema.

The institute will focus on practical industry training, professional mentorship, on-set learning experiences, and real-world exposure to film production.

Founded on the creative experience of filmmaker, actor, director, and producer Nav Bajwa, the school aims to bridge the gap between education and the entertainment industry by providing specialized training in:

A Vision by Nav Bajwa

Students will have the opportunity to learn from industry professionals and gain insights into contemporary filmmaking practices that serve both regional and global audiences.
