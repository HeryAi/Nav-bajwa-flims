WHY NAV BAJWA FILMS

Entertainment With Global Reach

Nav Bajwa Films offers strong collaboration opportunities for brands looking to connect with Punjabi, North Indian, and global South Asian audiences through cinema, OTT content, music, and digital campaigns. Since its establishment in 2016, the company has developed films across multiple genres including romance, thriller, comedy, family drama, action, and science fiction.
