Global Distribution Opportunities

Nav Bajwa Films actively licenses its film and entertainment content across domestic and international markets, creating opportunities for broadcasters, OTT platforms, distributors, airlines, educational institutions, and digital media partners.

With a diverse catalog spanning feature films, family dramas, romantic thrillers, comedies, science-fiction adventures, and music content, the company offers flexible licensing solutions tailored to different territories and platforms.

Nav Bajwa Films has produced and released acclaimed Punjabi films including Ishqaa, Raduaa, Raduaa Returns, and Madhaniyan, with content reaching audiences worldwide through theatrical, television, digital, and ancillary distribution channels.
