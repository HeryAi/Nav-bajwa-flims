India & International Distribution

At Nav Bajwa Films, we believe that great stories deserve a global audience. Beyond creating compelling content, we actively collaborate with distributors, exhibitors, streaming platforms, broadcasters, and international partners to ensure our films reach audiences across multiple markets and platforms.

With a growing portfolio of successful Punjabi and Indian productions, Nav Bajwa Films has built strong industry relationships that enable effective theatrical, digital, satellite, and overseas distribution strategies.

Our focus is not only on maximizing reach but also on creating long-term value for every project we represent.
