TALENT MANAGEMENT

Building The Next Generation Of Creative Professionals

Since its establishment in 2016, Nav Bajwa Films has emerged as a creative platform that nurtures and collaborates with talented artists, writers, technicians, musicians, and filmmakers across Punjabi and Indian cinema.

Led by actor, director, writer, and producer Nav Bajwa, the company is committed to developing both established and emerging talent while creating content that connects with audiences globally.

Through long-term creative collaborations and professional mentorship, we help talented individuals grow their careers across theatrical, digital, television, and international platforms.

CREATIVE COLLABORATIONS

Talent Across Successful Productions

Nav Bajwa Films has collaborated with a diverse pool of actors, directors, cinematographers, editors, composers, and creative professionals through productions including Raduaa, Ishqaa, Kitty Party, Fauji Band, Raduaa Returns, and Madhaniyan.

These projects have provided opportunities for fresh performers alongside experienced industry professionals, helping strengthen the creative ecosystem of Punjabi cinema.

CAREER DEVELOPMENT

Long-Term Creative Partnerships

With a growing presence across theatrical releases, OTT platforms, television networks, and international markets, Nav Bajwa Films continues to invest in talent development and meaningful creative collaborations.

We actively support emerging filmmakers, performers, writers, and technicians who bring fresh perspectives and culturally rooted stories to audiences worldwide.
