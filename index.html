Our Projects

Since 2016, Nav Bajwa Films has been creating diverse and impactful cinematic content across Punjabi, Bollywood, and international markets. Our portfolio spans romantic dramas, comedy and family entertainers, action thrillers, science fiction, social-impact stories, music-driven productions, and global collaborations.

We are committed to delivering compelling stories that entertain, inspire, and connect audiences through strong characters, cultural authenticity, and high production values. From heartwarming romances and family-oriented comedies to suspenseful thrillers and action-packed adventures, our films are designed to engage viewers across generations.

As part of our forward-looking vision, we are actively developing science fiction and futuristic projects that explore technology, artificial intelligence, space, and human evolution. We also produce socially relevant and inspirational cinema that reflects real-life experiences while promoting meaningful conversations.

Rooted in Punjabi culture, our music and cultural productions celebrate heritage, traditions, and artistic expression for audiences worldwide. Through international co-productions, overseas filming initiatives, and strategic global partnerships, we continue expanding the reach of Punjabi and Indian cinema.

With a growing slate of innovative projects, Nav Bajwa Films remains dedicated to creative excellence, cultural storytelling, and global entertainment, building a legacy that connects audiences across languages, cultures, and borders while shaping the future of modern filmmaking.

About Us

Established in 2016, Nav Bajwa Films is a film production and entertainment company dedicated to creating compelling stories for audiences across Pollywood, Bollywood, and international markets. Founded with a vision to elevate Punjabi and Indian cinema beyond traditional boundaries, the company has been actively developing and producing content that combines strong storytelling, cultural authenticity, and global appeal.

OUR VISION

To become a globally recognized entertainment company that bridges Pollywood, Bollywood, and international cinema, creating stories that inspire, entertain, and connect audiences worldwide.

OUR MISSION

To develop and produce exceptional film and digital content, foster international partnerships, promote cultural exchange, and contribute to the growth of the global entertainment industry through innovative storytelling.
