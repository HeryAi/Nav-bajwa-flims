Where Creativity Meets Opportunity

At Nav Bajwa Films, we are passionate about creating impactful Punjabi and Hindi cinema with a global vision. Since 2016, the company has been involved in film production, direction, writing, content development, and international distribution, with projects spanning drama, romance, thriller, courtroom drama, comedy, family entertainment, and science fiction.

WHO WE LOOK FOR

We welcome passionate individuals with strong creative vision, technical expertise, professionalism, and a commitment to cinematic excellence. Whether you are an experienced industry professional or an emerging talent, we value innovation, dedication, and the ability to work in a collaborative production environment.
