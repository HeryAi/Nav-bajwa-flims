Since 2016, Nav Bajwa Films has been creating high-quality Punjabi cinema with global appeal through innovative storytelling, strong leadership, and a vision to connect regional cinema with worldwide audiences.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

Building Punjabi Cinema For The World

Nav Bajwa Films Private Limited is a dynamic Punjabi film production company based in Punjab, India. Founded by Navjot Singh Bajwa, the company focuses on blending strong storytelling with modern filmmaking techniques while celebrating Punjabi culture, music, and entertainment.

Through innovative productions and genre-diverse projects, the company continues to expand the reach of Punjabi cinema beyond traditional boundaries.
